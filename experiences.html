Experiences

Days,
yours to shape.

Everything on the estate is offered, never imposed. Ride at dawn, or don't rise until noon. The team is on hand to arrange whatever your stay calls for.

— 01
Horseback

Riding out.

Our stable of forty horses caters to every level — gentle ponies for first-time riders, seasoned mounts for cantering across the plains. Guided rides of one hour to full days, with sundowners waiting at a favourite viewpoint.

Children are welcome in the arena; serious riders can bring their own saddles.

— 02
ATV & 4x4

Across the ridgeline.

Take a quad bike or a vintage Land Rover across the farm roads that wind up to the escarpment. Routes range from family-friendly loops around the dam to serious off-road climbs with Rift Valley panoramas at the top.

Helmets and instruction provided. Guided by estate rangers.

— 03
Walking Safaris

On foot, quietly.

Our rangers lead walking safaris across the ranch's acacia woodland, home to giraffe, zebra, eland, and the occasional leopard. Early mornings bring the best light and the most wildlife.

Half-day and full-day walks available.

— 04
Farm Life

Hands on the land.

Join the cattle move at sunrise. Collect eggs from the hen house. Pick vegetables for dinner from the kitchen garden. For families, these are often the moments children remember longest.

— 05
Day Excursions

Beyond the gates.

We'll arrange trips to Lake Nakuru for flamingos and rhino, a boat on Lake Naivasha, or a day's hike in the Aberdare forest. Private drivers and naturalist guides available.

Wellbeing

Unhurried, by design.

A visiting massage therapist, yoga on the veranda, books from the library, a bath drawn with lavender from the garden. The best days here often ask very little of you.
