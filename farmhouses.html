The Farmhouses

Two houses.
Yours alone.

Each farmhouse is booked as a whole — never shared. Come as a family, a gathering of friends, or simply a couple wanting the run of a whole house. Both homes come staffed with a housekeeper and a private chef.

House One

The Main House

A 1930s colonial farmhouse with deep verandas on three sides, five bedrooms, and an open stone fireplace large enough to stand in. The dining room seats twelve; the library holds a century of farming records and first editions.

Outside, a small heated pool sits within the walled garden, looking out toward the stables.

House Two

The Cottage

A more intimate stone cottage set beside the paddocks, with four en-suite bedrooms arranged around a central sitting room. The kitchen opens directly onto a herb garden; mornings begin with horses grazing outside the window.

Ideal for smaller parties, or booked together with The Main House for exclusive use of the whole estate.
