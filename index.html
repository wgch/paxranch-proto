Welcome

An estate for those who seek stillness.

Nestled in the highlands of Gilgil, Pax Ranch House offers an intimate kind of luxury — two fully private farmhouses set across a working horse and cattle ranch, available exclusively to one family or party at a time. No lobby. No neighbours. Only the land, the horses, and the quiet.

The Farmhouses

Two houses. One estate. Entirely yours.

Choose between The Main House — a five-bedroom colonial farmstead with wraparound veranda — or The Cottage, a four-bedroom retreat tucked beside the paddocks. Each is taken as a whole, ensuring your party is the only one in residence.

Open fireplaces, linen-dressed beds, and kitchens stocked from the estate garden. A housekeeper and chef are on hand; the pace is yours to set.

The Ranch

A working farm, open to those who stay.

Ride out at dawn through fever-tree woodland. Take an ATV across the ridgeline to watch the mist rise off Lake Elmenteita. Help bring the cattle in at dusk — or simply sit on the veranda and let the day pass.

Horses and ATVs are yours for the asking, with guides who know every track on the land.
